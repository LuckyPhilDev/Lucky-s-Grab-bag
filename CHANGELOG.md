## [1.2.0] - 2026-04-17

### Added
- Gear is now automatically repaired when you open a repair vendor. Uses guild funds by default if your guild allows it, with an option to always use your own gold instead. Both the feature and the guild funds preference can be toggled in settings.

### Fixed
- Multiple treatises now reliably withdraw from the Warband Bank in one go, instead of needing you to close and reopen the bank between each one.
